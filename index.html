About Mata Vaishno Devi Shrine Board

Shri Mata Vaishno Devi shrine is one of the most revered Hindu temples dedicated to Shakti, located in the Trikuta Mountains within the Indian Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir.

The shrine is situated at an altitude of 5,200 feet above sea level. Millions of devotees visit the shrine every year, making it one of the most visited religious destinations in India.
