Pilgrim Facilities

Complete guide to amenities and services available

Accommodation Facilities

Bhawan Area

Premium accommodation near the holy cave with modern amenities.

  • AC & Non-AC rooms
  • Attached bathrooms
  • 24/7 hot water
  • Room service
₹800 - ₹2000 per night
Adhkuwari

Mid-way accommodation facility for pilgrims taking rest during the journey.

  • Dormitory & private rooms
  • Basic amenities
  • Clean facilities
  • Safe storage
₹300 - ₹800 per night
Katra Base

Various accommodation options in Katra town before starting the pilgrimage.

  • Hotels & guest houses
  • Budget to luxury options
  • Restaurant facilities
  • Transportation hub
₹500 - ₹5000 per night
